# House Cleaning Tips for San Diego Homeowners

## Why Regular Cleaning Matters in San Diego

San Diego homes face unique challenges: coastal humidity, year-round dust from dry seasons, and heavy foot traffic during the outdoor-lifestyle months. A consistent cleaning routine keeps your home fresh and extends the life of your surfaces.

## Top Tips

**Steam cleaning** is the most effective method for San Diego homes. The 275°F thermal shock kills bacteria and removes built-up grime without harsh chemicals.

**Bathroom tiles** in coastal homes accumulate mineral deposits from hard water. Weekly wiping prevents buildup that requires expensive professional remediation.

**Kitchen hoods** in San Diego kitchens run year-round due to the climate — clean filters monthly to prevent fire hazards and maintain air quality.

## Frequency Guide

For most San Diego households, a recurring clean every two weeks maintains hygiene without over-investing in professional services. Homes near the beach or with pets may benefit from weekly attention.
